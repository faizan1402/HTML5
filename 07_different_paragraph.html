What is Computer Networks?


Computer networks are the basis of communication in IT. They are used in a huge variety of ways and can include many different types of network. A computer network is a set of computers that are connected together so that they can share information. The earliest of examples ofcomputer networks are from the 1960s, but they have come a long way in the half-century since then.

Local Area Networks (LAN)


A local area network or LAN is a network that connects computers within a limited area. This might be in a school, an office or even a home.
